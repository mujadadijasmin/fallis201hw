How to Make Thanksgiving Dinner

Here is How I Make Mine

Thanksgiving dinner is one of my favorite meals to make. It includes turkey, mashed potatoes, gravy, stuffing, and rolls. Below is a short step-by-step guide.

Step 1: Prepare the Turkey

Season the turkey with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and butter. Cook it in the oven at 350°F until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
